Golden Sands

Location

Golden Sands is the largest resort on the northern Black Sea coast. It is situated 18 km northeast away from Varna and 488 km east from Sofia in an ecologically clean bay. The complex is built in the Nature Park "Golden Sands" at 1320 ha. area. To the north it reaches the village of Kranevo and to the south - Chayka village.
